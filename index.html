Welcome to the Community Workshop on Grand Challenges in Wired Networking (2022-2023). This workshop will bring together researchers from computer networking, computer architecture, operating systems, distributed systems, computer security, and theory to discuss, identify and document the “long-term” research directions in wired networks. The key goal is to have academic and industry researchers and practitioners begin a collaborative discussion so as to build a more reliable, more robust, and more efficient next-generation computer network infrastructure.

This webpage contains all the relevant information for the workshop including the schedule, list of attendees, and other necessary logistics.
